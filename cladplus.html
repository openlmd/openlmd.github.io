Closed-Loop Process Control

The closed-loop control solution bases on high-speed SWIR/MWIR coaxial monitoring. Using a cost effective camera provided by NIT Europe which is able to provide high-speed thermal information with an uncooled solution in the spectral range from 1um to 5 um.

Software shared in OpenLMD is intended for research. Contact with AIMEN for an industrial implantation.


Closed-loop control interface for laser cladding.

Introduction

A simple PI controller is fed by the geometrical measurement calculated from the MWIR image. This measurements assumes that the melt pool can be estimated as an elliptical shape.
